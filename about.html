About Us

What is GFE?

GFE is a local platform that connects people who need everyday help with trusted workers in their area. From cleaning and childcare to elderly support and other small tasks, GFE makes it easy to find reliable help or flexible work within your community..

Our vision

To create a safe, user-friendly platform that brings communities together through everyday support.

Our mission

We aim to make local support more accessible, simple, and trustworthy for everyone.
